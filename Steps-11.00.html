Top 5 Violent Crimes in Chicago Bar Chart 11

This is what your finished chart should look like

1.         Drag a bar chart object and drop it on the sheet

2.         Create a dimension master item for Crime type

3.         Click on add dimension and select the dimension you just created



4.         Create measure for the violent crime percentage


5.         Change chart title


6.         Change the limitation to 5 and uncheck Others. Change the number formatting to number and choose the one with percentage with no decimal points.



7.         Click on Appearance then click on Presentation to edit the appearance of the chart. Click on Horizontal to change the charts alignment. Change the Scrollbar option to none so remove the Mini Chart.


8.         Changed the color to match the KPIs


9.  Changed  position of the label by clicking on X-axis and selecting Top for the position
