Creating Violent Crime and unsolved crime KPI. 10

Now we need to create a report with visuals as seen in the picture below. We want to showcase the area that has the most violent and unsolved crime. The page is divided into a logo on top right corner and a title top left corner . We also have three filters, one for years and one for crime type and one for area Also, Clear Button. Then we have two KPI showing Violent Crime rate and unsolved Crime Rate followed by 3 charts showcasing the area with the good and worst crime Areas.


Below is a picture of the complete product of one of our main KPIs.

The following steps will show you how we were able to obtain this chart with all the layers 

The first step of our process is to download an extension that can allow us to add more functionalities then the national KPI.

The extension in this case is formally known as the Climber KPI and can be found under the name Vizlib KPI Designer in the Vizlib library. We have included all the necessary Qlik Sense extension folders

Once the files have been downloaded and unzipped, we need to move all the folders into our Qlik Sense Extension folder. Below is my computer path to the extension folder, yours might be different:

                  C:\Users\[Your computer Name]\Documents\Qlik\Sense\Extensions                                     

Once the files are moved into the folder, we can go to our qlik app, refresh it and find the extension under Edit mode and in the Custom Objects

Now that we have our extension, we can follow the next steps:

1. Drag and drop a Vizlib KPI Designer from the library we just installed

2. Add the layers for onto the KPI:

- Layers are just elements/visuals that we used to make the KPI more informative and user friendly.

- We used a total of 4 layers for our KPIs: A Text Layer, an Icon, a Trend, and Master Items

The following steps will show you how we add a few layers:

                        There are 3 ways we can add a layer unto this extension:

                                               - From the properties panel

                                               - Drag and drop from master items

                                               - Right click on the actual object

a.  Add a Text Layer

b.  Add Icon Layer

c. Add Trend Layer

d. Add a Year Dimension and Violent Crime Rate measure to a trend layer

e. Add Shaded area

f. Add Area Color

g. Add a Violent Crime rate master items

 h. Format the numbers

I.  Change Font Color


By following the above steps you should be able to replicate the visuals for Violent Crime and Unsolved Crime  KPI.

After all the layers have been added you can edit them through the Properties Panel. However, Its worth mentioning that the Vizlib KPIs are a little sensitive/difficult to work with. So you have to exercise patience when working with them.
