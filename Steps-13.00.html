·       Violent Crime (Avg Violent Crime) on the x-axis

·       Unsolved Crime (Avg Unsolved Crime) on the y-axis



·   A drill down dimension :

·       SideCommunityDD (Side and Community area)

·       We used four colors here:

·       Green – for Lower Violent Crime area or in the lower left quadrant

·       Yellow – for avg Violent Crime area or in the top left quadrant

·       Red – for higher Violent Crime area or in the top left quadrant

·       Orange – for everything else or bottom right quadrant
